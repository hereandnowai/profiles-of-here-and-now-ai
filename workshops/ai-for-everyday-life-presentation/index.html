Prompt Engineering

The art of crafting effective prompts to get better AI responses

1

Role Assignment

Define who the AI should be

"You are an expert teacher" | "Act as a senior developer" | "You are a marketing consultant"
2

Define the Task

Be specific about what you want

"Create a presentation" | "Write a report" | "Debug this code" | "Analyze data"
3

Provide Context

Give background information

"For a beginner audience" | "This is for enterprise clients" | "Using Python 3.11"
4

Expected Format

Specify output structure

"In bullet points" | "As a table" | "Step-by-step guide" | "JSON format"
5

Constraints

Set boundaries and limits

"Under 200 words" | "No jargon" | "Use only free tools" | "Complete in 5 steps"
