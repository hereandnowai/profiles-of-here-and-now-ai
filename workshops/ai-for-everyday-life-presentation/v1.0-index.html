Understanding the Basics

AI (Artificial Intelligence)

The umbrella term for machines mimicking human intelligence.

ML (Machine Learning)

Computers learning from data without being explicitly programmed.

DL (Deep Learning)

Neural networks that mimic the human brain.

GenAI (Generative AI)

AI that creates new content (text, images, code).

AI Agent

Autonomous systems that use tools (Context, Tools, Database) to perform tasks.
